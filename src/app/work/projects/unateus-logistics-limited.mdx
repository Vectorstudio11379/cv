## Overview

As a Call Centre Agent at Unateus Logistics Limited, I was responsible for handling customer inquiries, providing detailed product information, and supporting the logistics team in daily operations. My role required strong communication skills, problem-solving abilities, and a customer-centric approach.

## Key Features

- **Customer Interaction:** Managed inbound and outbound calls, addressing customer questions and concerns.
- **Product Information:** Provided accurate and timely information about logistics services and products.
- **Issue Resolution:** Assisted customers in resolving issues and coordinated with the logistics team for prompt solutions.
- **Data Entry:** Maintained records of customer interactions and updated the CRM system.

## Technologies Used

- CRM Software: For managing customer data and tracking interactions.
- Telephony Systems: For handling calls efficiently.
- Office Tools (e.g., Google Workspace): For documentation and communication.

## Challenges and Learnings

A key challenge was managing high call volumes while maintaining quality service. I learned to prioritize tasks, stay organized, and communicate effectively under pressure. This experience improved my multitasking and customer service skills.

## Outcome

My contributions led to improved customer satisfaction, streamlined communication, and more efficient logistics operations at Unateus Logistics Limited.
