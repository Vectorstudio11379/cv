## Overview

Managed and enhanced Beacon Corporate Realty's online presence by ensuring website functionality, implementing SEO strategies, and producing engaging visual content, including marketing videos and aerial drone footage.

## Key Features

- **Website Management:** Ensured smooth functionality and user experience of the company website.
- **SEO Implementation:** Implemented SEO strategies to improve visibility and troubleshoot technical issues.
- **Video Editing:** Edited videos for marketing, property tours, and promotional purposes.
- **Drone Operation:** Operated drones to capture aerial images and videos of properties.

## Technologies Used

- **Web Platforms:** For managing website content and user experience.
- **SEO Tools:** For optimizing search engine visibility.
- **Video Editing Software:** For producing high-quality video content.
- **Drone Technology:** For capturing aerial footage.

## Challenges and Learnings

A key challenge was integrating diverse digital marketing strategies (website management, SEO, video, and drone operations) to create a cohesive and effective online presence. Learning to troubleshoot technical issues efficiently while continuously optimizing for user experience and search engine visibility was crucial.

## Outcome

The initiatives led to improved website functionality, increased online visibility through enhanced SEO, and more engaging promotional content. This has contributed to a stronger brand image and better showcasing of properties for Beacon Corporate Realty.
